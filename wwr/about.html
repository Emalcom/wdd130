River Runner

Mission; Is to give our guests' nature experiences of a life time with waterrapids, high speed river banks and nice lake scenaries. A vision; To make water experiences less scaring but fun. Moto; waterrapids your life long friend.

History

River Runner was started in 2010, by two brother Henry Willy and Eric Willy after the beautiful water experience at Albert water lakesides, falling in love with water rapids.
